Part number:       common-ibc v0.5

General fabrication notes

    * Boards are be individually routed.
    * Board material is Shengyi S1000-2M
    * No specific requirements on prepreg or core thickness.
    * Outer layer copper 2oz, inner layer 1oz
    * Blue solder mask on both sides of board.
    * White silkscreen text on both sides of board.
    * Pad finish is ENIG.
    * Finished board thickness is 1.6 mm.
    * Place UL mark and date code in the area marked on the "B. Fab" gerber layer.

File naming
    common-ibc-Edge_Cuts.gbr   Board outline
    common-ibc.drl             Through-board plated holes
    common-ibc-F_SilkS.gbr     Front silkscreen
    common-ibc-F_Mask.gbr      Front solder mask
    common-ibc-F_Cu.gbr        Layer 1 copper
    common-ibc-In1_Cu.gbr      Layer 2 copper
    common-ibc-In2_Cu.gbr      Layer 3 copper
    common-ibc-B_Cu.gbr        Layer 4 copper
    common-ibc-B_Mask.gbr      Back solder mask
    common-ibc-B_SilkS.gbr     Back silkscreen
    common-ibc-B.Fab.gbr       Shows requested location of date code and UL marking
    common-ibc.d356            Electrical test netlist
